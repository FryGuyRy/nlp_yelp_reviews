It was a little hard to find. It's down a walkway that can't be accessed by cars, along with about 4-5 other restaurants. you have the option of sitting inside or outside on the patio. Bread is not served, unless you ask, and I'm guessing you will be charged. We had the carbonara and a simple tomato and basil pasta. Both were very good and very hot (heat). One pasta is enough to feed to people so if you want to save room for dessert, just order one entree. The pizzas are medium sized and in our case would have probably been enough for two as well. Great ambience. The only set back is the musing blaring from the French bar next door. You may not run into this during the day or weekday evenings. We went on a Friday evening. Prices are very reasonable and they do speak English as well as German.
